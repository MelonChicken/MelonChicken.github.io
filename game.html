Under the Hood

Pinch Detection Swipe Velocity 21 Landmarks Real-time CV

Pinch: measures distance between thumb tip (LM 4) and index tip (LM 8) — triggers at <6.5% of canvas width.

Swipe: tracks wrist (LM 0) x-velocity over 8 frames — fires when displacement exceeds 15% of canvas width in <350 ms. The same velocity-estimation technique is used in animal behavior analysis.
