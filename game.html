How to Play

  1. Click Start Game and allow camera access
  2. Hold up your hand in front of the camera
  3. Point your index finger at falling bubbles to catch them
  4. Catch combos in a row for bonus points!
  5. You have 3 lives — don't let bubbles escape

Tech Under the Hood

MediaPipe Hands 21 Landmarks Real-time CV TensorFlow.js

This game uses Google's MediaPipe to detect 21 3D hand landmarks at ~30 fps entirely in your browser — no server, no data upload. The same landmark-tracking technique underpins research in gesture recognition and animal behavior analysis.
