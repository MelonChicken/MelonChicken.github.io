## Summary

Notes from running SuperAnimal inference and comparing HRNet vs RTMPose, plus where tracking fails.

## Problem / Question

Which backbone gives usable keypoints on real animal clips, and where does it break?

## What I tried

SuperAnimal quadruped inference; HRNet vs RTMPose; video_adapt fine-tuning.

## What I found

RTMPose was faster; HRNet steadier under partial occlusion. Motion blur is the common failure mode.

## Limitation

No behavior labels yet — this is still representation work.

## Next Step
