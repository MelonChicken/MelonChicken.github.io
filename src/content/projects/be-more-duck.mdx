## Problem

라즈베리파이 기반 동물 행동 관찰 반응형 에이전트

## Motivation

Connects animal-behavior interest to embodied AI / VLA, inspired by EPFL MOBOTS and Gemini Robotics.

## Method

Raspberry-Pi perception loop with a local VLM (Ollama/Llama) for behavior-conditioned responses.

## System / Pipeline

camera → VLM perception → behavior cue → agent response
